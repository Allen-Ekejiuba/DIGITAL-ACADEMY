FAQs

Common Questions

When does the next cohort start?

New cohorts begin every quarter (January, April, July, October). Contact us to confirm upcoming intake dates.

Are payment plans available?

Yes! We offer flexible instalment plans spread over the duration of the course. Speak to our admissions team for details.

Can I study online?

Most programmes are available both in-person and online. Our virtual classes use live video sessions and recorded materials.

Do you offer corporate training?

Absolutely. We provide bespoke corporate upskilling programmes for teams of all sizes. Contact us for a custom quote.
