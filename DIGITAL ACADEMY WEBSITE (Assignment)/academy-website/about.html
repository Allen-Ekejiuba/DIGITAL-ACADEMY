Who We Are

A Decade of Digital Excellence

Founded in 2015, the Digital Skills Training Academy (DSTA) was established with a clear mission: to bridge the digital divide in Nigeria and across Africa by providing world-class, affordable technology education.

We believe that access to quality digital education should not be a privilege. That's why we've designed our programmes to be accessible, flexible, and deeply practical — giving every student the skills they need to succeed in the modern workforce.

Over the past decade, we have trained more than 5,000 graduates, partnered with over 120 industry employers, and expanded from a single training centre in Lagos to a network of campuses across Nigeria.

What Drives Us

Mission & Vision

🎯

Our Mission

To provide accessible, industry-relevant digital skills training that empowers individuals to build meaningful careers and contribute to Nigeria's growing digital economy. We commit to hands-on learning, expert mentorship, and career-focused outcomes for every student.

🌍

Our Vision

To become Africa's most impactful digital skills institution — a place where ambition meets opportunity. We envision a future where every African professional has the technical literacy to compete on the global stage and drive innovation from the continent.
